Serving Friends and Family for Generations!

Whether they stand on two legs or four!

Fefe’s Cafe is a dog friendly beachfront cafe that aims to serve the community and their beloved pets. Fefe’s has an abundance of pet friendly treats available for purchase in our bakery and café. We have a full staffed kitchen as well as a bakery with an espresso bar! Fefe’s has plenty of fun games and toys for your pups and great ambiance. Whether you are strolling on the beach and want to stop in for some refreshments or craving a tasteful night out with your pals, Fefe’s Café is guaranteed to provide an unforgettable experience!
